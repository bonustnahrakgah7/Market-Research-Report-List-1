<p><p>The Customized Gas Permeable Contact Lenses market consists of three primary types: spherical, toric, and multifocal lenses. Spherical lenses are designed for individuals with regular vision needs, providing consistent optical correction. Toric lenses are tailored for astigmatism, featuring varying powers to correct different meridians of the eye. Multifocal lenses cater to presbyopia, offering multiple focal points to enable clear vision at varying distances. Each type meets specific vision correction requirements, enhancing comfort and visual clarity for wearers.</p></p>
