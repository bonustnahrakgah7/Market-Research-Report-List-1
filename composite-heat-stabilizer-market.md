<p><p>The composite heat stabilizer market is primarily divided into two types: metal soap stabilizers and organic heat stabilizers. Metal soap stabilizers, typically based on calcium and zinc compounds, provide efficient heat resistance and improved thermal stability, making them suitable for various plastic applications. In contrast, organic heat stabilizers, derived from organic compounds, offer advantages like lower toxicity and better environmental compatibility. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the longevity and performance of polymer products in different industries.</p></p>
