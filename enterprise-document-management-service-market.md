<p><p>The Enterprise Document Management Service market is segmented into three main types: On-Premise, Cloud-Based, and Hybrid solutions. On-Premise services involve installing document management systems locally on the organization's servers, offering greater control but requiring substantial IT resources. Cloud-Based services provide access to document management systems through the internet, offering scalability, lower initial costs, and reduced IT burden. Hybrid solutions combine both approaches, enabling organizations to leverage on-premise control while utilizing cloud flexibility for storage and collaboration needs.</p></p>
